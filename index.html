FAQ

Questions answered

Do I get the original artwork back?
Yes, always. Your child's originals are precious — I return every single piece once the collage is complete. Only high-quality scans and carefully selected originals are used in the final piece.
How many pieces of artwork do I need to send?
There's no strict minimum or maximum. Most commissions use between 6 and 20 pieces. I'll guide you on what works best for the size you've chosen during our initial chat.
How long does it take?
Typically 3–4 weeks from receiving the artwork to delivery. If you need it for a specific date or occasion, let me know upfront and I'll do my best to accommodate you.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! I ship worldwide. International shipping is quoted separately depending on the destination and size. Popular destinations include the USA, Australia, UAE, and Europe.
Can I give this as a gift without the child knowing?
Absolutely — it's actually one of the most popular ways to commission a piece. I can liaise with a parent or grandparent discreetly, and deliver directly to the gift-giver.
What types of artwork can you work with?
Drawings, paintings, watercolours, handprints, collages, handwriting, doodles — anything your child has made. The more variety, the richer the finished piece.
